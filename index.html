🎨 Bring this video into Canva

  1. Tap Save Video in the app first — this downloads a .webm file to your phone.
  2. If Canva has trouble opening .webm on your device, save the video to your phone's gallery first — most phones auto-convert it to .mp4 when you do this, which Canva always accepts.
  3. Open the Canva app → Create a design → pick any video template or a blank video.
  4. Tap Uploads → Upload media → select your saved video file.
  5. Drag it onto your timeline at the bottom of the screen.

Once it's on Canva's timeline, you have full control:

  1. Split it into pieces (tap the clip → Split) to edit individual scenes.
  2. Trim the start/end of any piece by dragging its edges.
  3. Add your own text, stickers, music, or transitions on top.
  4. Reorder pieces by dragging them on the timeline.
Canva won't know which clip was "scene 3" from your script — it just sees plain video. If you want to edit scenes separately from the start, generate and save shorter clips scene-by-scene instead of the full video, then arrange them yourself in Canva.
